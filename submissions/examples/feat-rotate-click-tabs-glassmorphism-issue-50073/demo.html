ease-rotate-click-tabs-glassmorphism

Pure CSS tabs — frosted glass surface, rotate-click panel transitions, zero JavaScript.

Explore

Tab, or use Arrow keys once focused, to move between tabs — the underlying <input type="radio"> group handles all of it natively, no JavaScript involved.

Create

On click, the incoming panel rotates in from a slight tilt while fading up, then settles flat — a subtle 3D "click" flourish over a frosted glass surface.

Share

Tune --ease-tabs-glass-duration, --ease-tabs-glass-easing, and --ease-tabs-glass-rotate to change the feel of the rotation.
